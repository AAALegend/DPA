Rather than calling it cooking, its just enough to make stuff edible. Dont look down on life on the streets.

However, even though she was only eating at a slow pace, she was still eating quite well.

Wow.

Yeah, although I saw this phenomenon all the time, it even surprised me all the time. Sometimes I felt like punching her for being so shameless to eat almost everything by herself and then looking like she ate nothing at all.

You seem to be very close. Do you eat like this every night?

Yeah. Wed usually have a meal around this time after I got beaten up enough.

Harriet had a startled expression plastered on her face when she heard me talk about fighting. Well, she had no reason to visit the training room, so she wouldnt have any idea what was going on in there every day.

She probably thought I wasnt the type to be beaten up even if someone were to hit me, but now she heard that I got beat up by Ellen every day. Her eyes oozed disbelief.

Are you only drinking tea right now so that you can eat these cookies?

Thats right.

No way.

At first, he gave me a letter.

Dont say it!

Tha, thats right. W, we take swordsmanship classes together, and because of that duel the other day, erm, I asked her to help me with my training, so she did. Yeah, thats how it went!
