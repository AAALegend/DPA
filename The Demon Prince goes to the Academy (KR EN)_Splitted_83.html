82 화

굳이 샬롯이 나와 함께 여기에 온 이유가 있었다.

There was a reason why Charlotte had to take me with her.

“뭔가 느껴지면 말해줘.”

“Tell me if you feel something.”

“어…. 될지는 모르겠지만.”

“Ah…. I don’t really know if anything like that will happen though.”

내 초능력이 무슨 기적을 일으킬지 모르니 일단 무작정 데려온 것이다. 발리에가 마지막으로 있었던 장소에 있다 보면 내가 단서를 얻을지도 모 르니까.

She didn’t know what kind of miracles my supernatural power could work, so she brought me here on a whim. Maybe I would be able to give her a clue about Valier’s whereabouts if I visit the place he was last seen. That was probably what she was thinking.

샬롯은 아마 여기에서 얻을 수 있 는 정보는 이미 다 얻어냈을 것이다.

Charlotte probably already had all the information she could get out of this place.

하지만 자신이 직접 확인해보고픈 것도 있고，나를 데려오면 뭔가 달라 질지도 모르니 직접 온 것 같았다.

However, there still seemed things she wanted to check out personally, and she thought, if she brought me, things might change, so that led to this situation.

“이렇게 생긴 사람 본 적 있으세요?”

“Have you ever seen someone that looks like this?”

샬롯은 여기저길 돌아다니며 발리 에의 초상화를 들고 탐문을 시작했 다. 내가 스크롤 상점을 돌아다녔다 는 건 이미 알고 있을 텐데도 샬롯 은 꼬박꼬박 상점가를 배회하며 이 것저것 묻고 다녔다.

Charlotte wandered around, carrying Valier’s portrait, and began questioning people. I was pretty sure that she already went around the scroll shops, but Charlotte still went ahead and wandered around the shopping street asking passers-by.

본 적이 있느냐，본 적이 있다면 어디로 간지 아느냐.

Have you seen him? If you have, do you know where he went?

물론 여긴 알리가르 상점가다. 돈 내는 놈 아니면 험한 취급 받기 십 상인 곳.

Of course, this was still the Aligar District shopping street. A place where you’d earn hatred if you didn’t spend any money.

“애들이 여긴 웬 일이냐? 구경은 안 돼. 나가라.”

“What are you brats doing here? You can’t look around. Scram.”

샬롯은 로브를 입고 있지만 체구가 작고，나도 척 보면 미성년자다. 모험 가를 할 녀석으론 보이지 않는 거다.

Charlotte was wearing a robe, but she was still very small, so one could see her as anything but a minor. I couldn’t see her as an adventurer either.

그래서 상대도 해주지 않았다. 구 경 같은 건 안 된다면서.

Neither did the person in front of her. He didn’t even allow us to take a look.

이 정도는 차라리 양반이었다.

How very gangster-like.

애새끼들이 돈 낼 것도 아니면서 부정 타게 들어오고 지랄이냐고 험 한 소리 해대는 이들 천지였다.

He let some choice words fly our way, saying stuff about some kids just wandering in willy-nilly without even buying anything. He even asked us if we were crazy.

자기가 그런 소릴 한 게 제국 1황 녀라는 걸 알면 혀 깨물고 죽고 싶 어질 말들의 향연이었다.

He sure had some fragrant words to offer. So fragrant that he’d immediately bite his tongue off if he knew he was directing them to the First Imperial Princess.

이럴 거면 평상복이 아니라 템플 교복이라도 입고 오는 건데. 그러면 돈이라도 많다고 생각했을 테니까.

In that case, we should have just worn our Temple Uniforms, not our casual clothes. That way they’d think there was big money to earn from us.

“…쉽지 않을 거란 건 알았는데 아 예 상대도 안 해주는 사람들 천지네.” 물론，샬롯은 한참을 돌아다니다가 문전박대당하는 것에 지친다는 듯 한숨을 푹푹 쉬어댔다.

“…I knew it was going to be tough, but there are a lot of people who don’t even want to listen to what I have to say.”

After walking around the place for quite some time and getting pushed out of store doors, she was obviously tired. Charlotte let out a sigh.

“……핀트가 좀 잘못된 것 같은데. 평민들한테 이런 소리 듣는 것에 일 단 화가 나야 하는 거 아냐?”

“……I feel like the point you’re focusing on is kind of wrong. Aren’t you supposed to get pissed off by commoners treating you like that?”

샬롯은 쫓아내면 쫓아내는 대로，상 대를 해주면 해주는 대로 이런저런 얘기를 했지만 화가 난 기색은 없었 다. 내 말에 샬롯은 맥없이 웃었다.

Charlotte did grumble a bit after she got kicked out and treated the way she was, but she didn’t seem angry. Hearing my words, Charlotte laughed helplessly.

“로브 뒤집어쓰고 있다가 험한 소 리 들었답시고 이 녀석이 어디 무엄 하게! 내가 누군지 아느냐! 이러면 그것도 참 웃기는 꼴이겠다.”

“He swore at me while I was in disguise. Wouldn’t it be really weird if I suddenly said something like ‘You peasant! Do you know who you are talking to’?”

일부러 정체를 감추고 다니는 건데 자길 몰라봤다고 화내는 건 대체 무 슨 짓거리냐는 듯，샬롯은 이 정도 는 충분히 각오하고 있는 것 같았 다. 지금 중요한 건 내 체면이나 명 예 따위가 아니라는 모양이다.

She was intentionally hiding her identity, so Charlotte seemed fully prepared for what kind of treatment she’d receive when no one was able to recognize her as the Princess. It seemed like what was important for her at the moment wasn’t her honor or pride.

자기 발로 직접 될 정도인 샬롯을 보아하니 얼마나 절박한지 피부로 느껴질 정도였다.

When I witnessed Charlotte getting back up on her own feet, I could feel just how desperate she was.

“뭔가 느껴지거나 이런 건 없어?”

“Can you sense anything?”

“……딱히.”

“……No.”

“역시….”

“As I thought….”

별로 기대는 안 했다는 듯 샬롯은 그리 실망한 기색은 아니었다.

As if she didn’t expect much from this, Charlotte didn’t seem very disappointed.

“가자.”

“Let’s go.”

샬롯은 아직 포기할 생각이 없어보 였다.

However, she didn’t seem to have any intention to give up just yet.

그나마 정보를 얻을 수 있는 곳은 스크롤 상점이었다. 다행히도 모두 가 문전박대를 하는 건 아니었다.

The only place one could get any information was at the scroll shops. Fortunately, not everyone was slamming their doors in our faces.

“대체 개가 뭐길래 이렇게 사람들 이 찾는 걸까? 개가 결국 그 가짜 스크롤로 큰 사고라도 친 거니?”

“Just who is that brat that everyone’s looking for ‘im? Did that brat cause some incident with those fake scrolls of his?”

나를 기억하는 스크롤 상점주인들 은 대개 그런 반응이었다. 가짜 스 크롤을 팔러 왔던 소년，그리고 그 이후 그 소년을 찾기 위해 여기저기 들쑤시고 다니는 일단의 사람들.

The Scroll Store owners who remembered me generally reacted like that. A boy who came to them to sell some fake scrolls, and that group of people who were rushing about looking for him ever since then.

내가 엄청나게 큰 범죄에 연루되었 다 생각하는 것 같았다.

They seemed to think that I got involved in some huge crime.

“경비대 비슷한 사람들이 막 물어 보고 다니더니 이젠 너 같은 애들까 지 찾아다니고 말이야.”

“People who looked like Guards just came around askin bout ‘im, and now some kids like you are after ‘im as well.”

이젠 별별 사람들이 다 찾아다닌다 며 상점주인은 이상하게 여기는 것 같았다.

The store owner seemed to find this situation very strange, saying that everyone suddenly started looking for that boy.

“자세한 건 말씀드리기 어렵지만 중 요한 문제예요. 그 아이에 대해서 생각 나는 건 전부 말해주실 수 있을까요?”

“I can’t tell you why we are looking for him, but it’s very important. Could you tell me everything you know about him?”

“내가 말해줄 건 딱히 없단다. 몇 달 전쯤에 스크롤북을 매고 다니는 웬 어린애가 스크롤을 팔려고 했어. 그런데 잘 그리긴 했는데 영 엉터리 스크롤이지 뭐니. 그래서 가짜 스크 롤을 팔고 다니는구나 싶어서 쫓아 냈다. 이게 끝이야.”

“There’s nothin’ much to it. A few months ago, a young boy came here tryin to sell some scrolls. Although they were very well-made, they were very shoddy. So I thought he was tryin to sell me fake scrolls. In the end I kicked him out. That’s all.”

“네…. 감사합니다.”

“Okay…. Thank you.”

“쯧，어린 것이 분명히 큰 사고라 도 친 모양이지. 그러게 사람은 정 직하게 돈벌이를 해야 하는 법이야. 너희들도 명심하렴.”

“Sigh, that brat must have gotten into some serious trouble. That’s why people should earn their money through honest means. You should be mindful of that as well.”

상점주인은 뜬금없이 나와 샬롯에 게 훈계를 했고，우리는 별 말 없이 상점을 나왔다.

The shopkeeper came forward and gave Charlotte a warning, then we left the store without saying a word.

“후우…. 결국 다 알던 내용들이라 서 딱히 다를 건 없네.”

“Sigh…. In the end, they only told me things I already knew. Nothing changed.”

직접 자기 귀로 들어도 보고받은 내용과 크게 다르지 않은 건 당연할 거다.

Even if one went to investigate and question the same witnesses personally, it was only natural that their testimony wouldn’t be any different from what was written in the reports.

“가짜 스크롤이니 뭐니 하는 얘기 가 뭐야?”

“What did they mean by fake scrolls?”

물론 나는 지금 상황에선 샬롯이 왜 여기에 온 건지，여기서 발리에 가 뭘 했던 건지 몰라야 했다. 샬롯 은 로브 속에서 날 쳐다보더니，작 게 입을 열었다.

Of course, I wouldn’t know why Charlotte was wandering around in this place and why Valier even came here with my current presumed level of knowledge. Charlotte looked at me from inside her robe and opened her mouth slightly.

“개가 마왕성에서 나와 함께 탈출 했다는 건 내가 말했지?”

“I told you that this boy escaped with me from the Demon King’s Castle, right?”

“네 목숨을 구해줬다고….”

“You said, he saved your life….”

“자세한 사정을 말해주긴 어려운 데，개는 나한테서 벗어나야 할 이 유가 있었어.”

“I can’t tell you everything, but this boy had his reasons for separating from me.”

베르투스의 보복 가능성 때문에 발 리에는 도망쳤어야 했다. 샬롯은 내 가 자길 떠난 이유를 알고 있었다. 물론 내가 마족이기 때문에 도망쳤 다는 건 전혀 모르는 것 같지만.

Because of the possible retaliation from Bertus, Valier fled. That’s what Charlotte believed to be the reason. Of course, she didn’t know that I actually ran from her because I was a demon.

“그런데 기억도 없고 아는 것도 없 는 애가 대체 뭘 할 수 있겠어. 무 작정 도망쳤는데, 앞길은 캄캄했겠 지. 그런데 개는 마왕성에서 가져온 마법 스크롤들이 있었단 말이야. 아 마 그걸 이 알리가르 상점가에 팔아 서 일단 돈을 충당하려고 했던 것 같아.”

“But what can a kid with amnesia and no proper knowledge do? He ran away recklessly, so the future seemed rather bleak at first. But he had some magic scrolls from the Demon King’s Castle. Perhaps that’s why he tried to raise some funds by selling them to the Scroll Shop owners on the Aligar District’s shopping street.”

샬롯은 밝혀진 사실들을 통해 진실 을 추론해내고 있었다. 웬 녀석이 가짜 스크롤을 팔고 다녔다. 거기에 서 샬롯은 그 가짜 스크롤이 마족의 스크롤이라는 것，그렇기에 그 소년 이 발리에가 확실하다는 결론을 얻 어냈을 거다.

Charlotte deduced the truth from only these bits of information. A boy who sold fake scrolls. From that, Charlotte might have concluded that those fake scrolls were actually demon scrolls, and that boy would be Valier without a doubt.

“그런데 마족의 스크롤과 우리들이 쓰는 스크롤은 구조 자체가 다르단 말이야. 마법사가 아닌 상인들이 보 기에는 그냥 장난인 줄 알거나 사기 를 치려 한다고 생각했겠지.”

“However, the structure of demonic scrolls are vastly different from the scrolls we use. Merchants who have no magician-background would probably think he was trying to mess with them, trying to sell them fake scrolls.”

“Ah…. Is that so?”

“아…. 그래?” 마족의 스크롤과 인간들이 쓰는 스 크롤은 구조 자체가 다르다. 그렇기 때문에 나는 멋모르고 마족 스크롤 을 팔아먹으려고 싸돌아다니다가 끝 내 엘레리스를 만날 수 있었다.

Demon scrolls and the one’s humans used had structural differences. Without that knowledge I ignorantly wandered in every scroll shop and tried to sell them before I finally met Eleris.

“그래서 결국 문제를 느끼고 마족 스크롤을 팔 수 없다는 걸 알아채서 인지 어떤지는 모르겠지만，한 상점 에서 파이어볼 스크롤을 흠쳤다고 들었어. 그런데 이상하지. 파이어볼 스크롤을 흠쳤다는 목격증언은 있는 데，그 파이어볼 스크롤을 산 사람 은 없단 말이야.”

“I don’t know whether he felt like something was wrong and realized that he wouldn’t be able to sell the demonic scrolls, but I heard he took a Fireball scroll from one of the stores to sell. That isn’t the weird part however. There were eyewitness testimonies that stated that he took a Fireball scroll, but no one ever bought one around that time.”

거기에선 엘레리스가 거짓증언을 했다. 그렇기에 단서는 거기에서 끊 겨버린 것이다.

Eleris falsified her testimony, so that was where the trace got cut off.

“흠친 다음 겁먹고 엄청 멀리 도망 가버린 거 아닐까?”

“Maybe he stole it, got scared and simply ran away.”

“그럴 수도 있겠어. 그러면 여기가 아니라 다른 곳 상점가를 뒤져봐야 하긴 할 텐데….”

“That’s possible. Then I would have to investigate shopping streets located in different places….”

내 의도적인 거짓정보에 샬롯은 그 럴 수 있다는 듯 고개를 끄덕였다.

Charlotte nodded her head after listening to my intentionally misleading comment.

“Let’s go to the store from where he stole the Fireball scroll.”

“일단 그 파이어볼 스크롤을 도둑 맞았다는 그 상점으로 가보자.” 물론 도둑질한 게 아니라 직접 건 네받은 거지만，굳이 내가 정정해줄 수는 없었다.

Of course, I didn’t actually steal it. That guy handed it over willingly, but I couldn’t correct her on this point.

샬롯은 나를 이끌고 앞서 걸었다. 이미 다 알고 있는 정보들일 텐데， 샬롯은 굳이 눈과 귀로 재확인하다 보면 뭔가를 알 수 있단 생각이라도 하는 걸까.

Charlotte walked ahead to lead the way. That must have been information she had already been aware of, so did she really think that she would get any different results just because she decided to investigate herself?

욕심에 눈이 멀어 파이어볼 스크롤 하나를 쌩으로 날린 그 놈의 상점에 도착했다.

Like that we arrived at the shop of this guy who gave me that Fireball scroll, blinded by greed.

여기에 내가 다시 오게 될 거라고 는 상상도 못 하고 있었는데.

I never thought I’d ever return to this place.

“……뭐야? 웬 애들이냐?”

“……What? What do you kids want?”

그 주인 남자，엘레리스가 이름을 말해줬는데 기억은 안 난다. 하지만 여전히 심술궂고 욕심 많아 보이는 인상이었다. 샬롯이 앞장서서 카운 터 쪽으로 걸어갔다.

Eleris told me this shop owner’s name, but I couldn’t remember it. He still looked as grumpy and greedy as before though. Charlotte took the lead and walked towards the counter.

“보르통 씨 맞으시죠?”

“Are you Mr. Borton?”

“……어，그런데?”

“……Yeah, so what?”

대뜸 웬 녀석이 자기 이름을 불러 오니 그는 조금 흠칫한 표정이 되었 다. 샬롯은 사전정보를 거의 다 파 악하고 온 것 같았다.

When she called out the guy’s name, he seemed slightly surprised. Charlotte seemed to have compiled almost every piece of information about him prior to this.

“일전에 어떤 녀석한테 파이어볼 스 크롤을 하나 도둑맞으셨다고 했죠?”

“You reported that someone stole a Fireball scroll from you the other day, is that correct?”

“……뭐야，또 그 얘기냐? 할 얘긴 다 했잖아? 이젠 애들까지 시켜서 알아보고 다니는 거냐?”

“……What? You’re asking about this again? I told those Guards everything I had to say. Are they even having kids look into this now?”

이 얘기로 주구장창 떠들어댔다는 듯，그는 이 이야기가 지긋지긋한 모양이었다.

It seemed like he was sick and tired of talking about this incident, as if he constantly had to repeat the story.

“죄송해요. 그래도 중요한 사건이 라서 계속 알아보고 있는 거거든요. 다시 한 번만 그때 상황이 어땠는지 알 수 있을까요?”

“I’m sorry to ask about this again. Still, this is very important to us, so we’re still looking into it. Could you tell me what happened back then one more time?”

샬롯은 권위를 앞세우기보단 일단 은 좋게 물어봤다.
