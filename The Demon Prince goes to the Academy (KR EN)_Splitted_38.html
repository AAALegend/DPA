베르투스는 킬킬거린다. 머리는 좋 고 판단력도 좋은데，시비가 붙으면 대뜸 주먹부터 나가는 또라이라고 날 평가하는 것 같았다. 그리고 내 가 거지 출신이라 그런 또라이 짓을 하는 것도 이해한 것 같고.

Bertus chuckled.  It seemed like he judged me as someone who was smart and had good judgement, but as soon as someone started to argue with me I would use my fist first. Since I was a beggar, he seemed to think that that was why I acted like that.

아니 그런데.

No, but….

I wasn’t actually a beggar….

“Well, what I wanted to say stays the same, in the end. Try to hold in your temper.”

원점으로 돌아와서 결국 같은 말을 한다.

We came back to the beginning and he ended up saying the same thing.

“노력은 해볼 텐데，애들이 말이 통해야 말이지.”

“I’ll try, but only if those brats can be communicated with.”

“뭐 그래. 일단은 그게 다야.”

“Well, yeah. That’s all for now.”

베르투스는 테라스 난간에서 내려 와선 날 마주보고 섰다.

Bertus came down form the terrace railing and stood in front of me.

“네가 내 사람이란 확신이 들었을 때，그 때부턴 좀 더 중요한 얘길 하자고.”

“When I’m sure you’re mine, let’s talk about some more important things then.”

베르투스가 날 신뢰할 시간도.

The time for Bertus to trust me.

내가 베르투스를 신뢰할 시간도.

The time for me to trust Bertus.

That time hadn’t come yet.

하지만 베르투스는 내 가치를 알아 봤고，그렇기에 먼저 떡밥을 던져놓 은 것이다. 네 가치를 먼저 알아본 게 누구인지 잊지 말라는 것처럼.

But Bertus did recognize my worth, which is why he threw out the bait first. Something like “Don’t forget who recognized your value first”.

누구보다 먼저인지는 굳이 생각하 지 않아도 알 수 있었다.

I knew without even thinking about it who came first.
